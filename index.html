The Art of Fragrance Since 1976

Crafting Emotions Through Scents

Founded in the heart of Paris in 1976, Sephora began as a dream to capture the essence of human emotion in a bottle. For nearly five decades, we have traveled the globe to source the rarest ingredients—from the hand-picked jasmine of Grasse to the deep, mysterious oud of the East.

We believe that a perfume is more than just a scent; it is your personal signature, a silent language that speaks before you do. Our curated collections are chosen for those who demand nothing less than extraordinary elegance.
